Tag Connection Privacy Policy

Tag Connection respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tag Connection we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information such as email address and login details
  • Profile information including photos, bio, and personal preferences
  • Messages exchanged between users
  • Usage data such as interactions with profiles, swipes, and connections
  • Device information such as device type, operating system, and app version
  • Location data used to help users find connections nearby

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the Tag Connection platform
  • Match users and enable connections
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Maintain safety and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Respond to user support requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. User Content

Content you upload such as photos, profile information, and messages may be visible to other users depending on how you use the platform.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

5. Age Restriction

Tag Connection is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly allow users under 18 to create accounts.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. Information may only be shared when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Needed to protect the safety of users
  • Necessary to operate the platform securely

7. Account Control

Users may update or delete their account information through the app. If you delete your account, associated data may be removed from our systems.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Tag Connection means you accept any updates.
