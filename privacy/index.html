Tag Connection Privacy Policy

Tag Connection respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tag Connection, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information (such as email address and login credentials)
  • Profile information (including photos, bio, and personal preferences)
  • Messages exchanged between users
  • Usage data (such as swipes, interactions, and connections)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, and app version)
  • Location data to help users find nearby connections

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the Tag Connection platform
  • Match users and enable connections
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Maintain safety and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Respond to support requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. User Content

Content you upload (such as photos, profile details, and messages) may be visible to other users, depending on how you use the platform.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no system is completely secure.

5. Age Restriction

Tag Connection is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly allow individuals under 18 to create accounts.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. Information may only be shared when:

  • Required by law or legal processes
  • Necessary to protect the safety of users
  • Required to operate and secure the platform

7. Account Control

Users can update or delete their account information within the app. If an account is deleted, associated data may be removed from our systems.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Tag Connection means you accept any updates.
