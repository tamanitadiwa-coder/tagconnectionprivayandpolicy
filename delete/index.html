Delete Your Account

Your Right

You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email: support@tagconnection.co
  2. Subject: "Delete my account"
  3. Include your account email or phone number

What Gets Deleted

  • Profile information (photos, bio, age)
  • Messages and chat history
  • Matches and connections
  • Login credentials

Data Retention

Some data may be temporarily retained for security and legal reasons, but will be permanently deleted within 30–90 days.

Important

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
